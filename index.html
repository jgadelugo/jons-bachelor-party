Bachelor Party Beer Olympics Schedule

9:00 AM – 9:30 AM: Be Ready downstairs

  • Split into 3 teams of 3 players each (Jon’s team may have a 4th member if there are 10 people).
  • Weakest Link Selection: Anonymous voting; votes are revealed one by one. The person with the most votes joins Jon’s team as the "Weakest Link."

9:30 AM – 10:00 AM: Team Country Selection and Opening Ceremony

  • Each team selects a country to represent.
  • Teams have a brief time to plan how they’ll embody their country (outfits, chants, etc.).
  • Quick presentations by each team.
  • Bonus Points: The team that best embodies their country wins 10 bonus points at the end.

10:00 AM – 11:00 AM: Race to Build a Fire (Boil Water in a Soda Can)

  • Each team receives a fire-starting kit.
  • Teams race to build a fire capable of boiling water in the soda can.
  • Teams that fail to boil water within 30 minutes must each shotgun a beer.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

11:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Kayak Relay Race

  • A course is set from one side of the lake to the other and back.
  • Teams play flip cup relay-style, then paddle a kayak across the lake and back.
  • Teams that fail must shotgun a beer.
  • Points: Same as above.

1:00 PM – 1:45 PM: Blind Beer/Whiskey Taste Test

  • Two players from each team taste the drink—one for whiskey, one for beer.
  • Players guess the type or brand after each sip; wrong guesses result in shots of beer.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

1:45 PM – 2:30 PM: Cocktail-Making Contest (Blind Reviews)

  • Teams create a cocktail within 10 minutes.
  • Blindfolded judges rate on taste, presentation, and creativity.
  • Points: Best overall score: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

2:30 PM – 3:15 PM: Civil War (Team Beer Pong)

  • Each player defends 3 cups.
  • Continuous play until one team remains.
  • Points: Same as above.

3:15 PM – 4:00 PM: Beer Frisbee Golf

  • Golf rules with a frisbee; missed targets result in beer sips.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

4:00 PM – 4:45 PM: Flip Cup Relay

  • Relay-style flip cup competition; half a beer per round.
  • Points: Same as above.

4:45 PM – 5:45 PM: Drunk Dizzy Bat Relay

  • Players chug beer, spin, and hit a can with a bat.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

5:45 PM – 6:30 PM: Rhino Pussy - Hot Sauce Competition

  • Players consume progressively hotter sauces; tapping out results in beer chugging.
  • Points: Same as above.

7:15 PM – 9:45 PM: Beer Pong Tournament & Beer Mile

  • Teams play beer pong; laps around the course with beer chugging after each lap.
  • Points: Same as above.

10:15 PM – 11:00 PM: Business Idea Contest

  • Teams brainstorm and pitch a business idea.
  • Points: Most likely to succeed: 7 points, creativity/presentation: 4 points.

11:00 PM – 11:30 PM: Awards and Penalties

  • The team with the least points jumps into the lake at midnight.
  • Winning team earns bragging rights and a ride in Rich’s Tesla.
