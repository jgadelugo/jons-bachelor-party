Bachelor Party Beer Olympics Schedule

9:00 AM – 9:30 AM: Be Ready downstairs

  • Split into 3 teams of 3 players each (Jon’s team may have a 4th member if there are 10 people).
  • Weakest Link Selection: Anonymous voting; votes are revealed one by one. The person with the most votes joins Jon’s team as the "Weakest Link."
  • Anonymous voting: Everyone writes a name on a piece of paper and places it in a hat. Votes are revealed one by one. The person with the most votes joins Jon’s team as the “Weakest Link.”
  • Each team keeps their beer cans, and builds a pyramid. At the end best pyramid gets 10 points

9:30 AM – 10:00 AM: Team Country Selection and Opening Ceremony

  • Each team selects a country to represent.
  • Teams have a brief time to plan how they’ll embody their country (outfits, chants, etc.).
  • Quick presentations by each team.
  • Bonus Points: The team that best embodies their country wins 10 bonus points at the end.

10:00 AM – 11:00 AM: Race to Build a Fire (Boil Water in a Soda Can)

  • Each team receives a fire-starting kit.(Wood, kidnle, lighter/matches, can)
  • Teams race to build a fire capable of boiling water in the soda can.
  • Teams that fail to boil water within 30 minutes must each shotgun a beer.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

11:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Kayak Relay Race

  • A course is set from one side of the lake to the other and back.(If we can...)
  • Teams play flip cup relay-style, when it gets to the chosen kayaker he can start going across the lake and back.
  • Last team must shotgun a beer.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

1:00 PM – 1:45 PM: Blind Beer/Whiskey Taste Test

  • Two players from each team taste the drink—one for whiskey, one for beer.
  • Players guess the type or brand after each sip; wrong guesses result in shots of beer.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

1:45 PM – 2:30 PM: Cocktail-Making Contest (Blind Reviews)

  • Teams create a cocktail within 10 minutes.
  • Blindfolded judges rate on taste, presentation, and creativity.
  • Points: Best overall score: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

2:30 PM – 3:15 PM: Civil War (Team Beer Pong)

  • Arrange three tables in a triangle or play round-robin style. Each player defends 3 cups.
  • Continuous play until one team remains.
  • Points: Best overall score: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.<

3:15 PM – 4:00 PM: Beer Frisbee Golf

  • Standard golf rules but with a frisbee everyone can play and we take the avg team score or we can select two from each team to play and lowest team score wins
  • Miss the target take a sip
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

4:00 PM – 4:45 PM:Beer Mile

  • One person starts by chugging or shotgunning a beer, then runs a lap.
  • Teams can switch runners at any lap; new runners must chug/shotgun a beer before running.
  • If no switching, remaining players must finish a beer for the team to finish.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

4:45 PM – 5:45 PM: Drunk Dizzy Bat Relay

  • Player chugs a beer and it is timed. For how many seconds it took the player must spin around a bat Max 10 times before a player from his team tosses him the empty can which he has to swing and hit.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

5:45 PM – 6:30 PM: Rhino Pussy - Hot Sauce Competition

  • One player consumes a spoonful of each sauce in increasing order. Must wait at least 2 minutes per sauce before moving on. Can’t drink or eat anything else.
  • can tap out at any time either by eating or drinking anything or by literally tapping out.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

7:15 PM – 8:45 PM: Beer Pong Tournament

  • TOption 1: Teams of 2 with 10-cup formation. Option 2: 1v1 with 6-cup formation. Jon’s House Rules apply.
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

8:45 PM – 9:45 PM: Relay

  • flip, bounce it in, then shoot it (think flip cup + slap cup + beer pong)
  • Points: 1st place: 3 points, 2nd place: 2 points, 3rd place: 1 point.

10:15 PM – 11:00 PM: Business Idea Contest

  • Teams brainstorm and pitch a business idea.
  • Points: Most likely to succeed: 7 points, creativity/presentation: 4 points.

11:00 PM – 11:30 PM: Awards and Penalties

  • The team with the least points jumps into the lake at midnight.
  • Winning team earns bragging rights, Armand de Brignac Champagne and rides in Rich’s Tesla
